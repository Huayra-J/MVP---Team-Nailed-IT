Importance of Quoting, Paraphrasing and Summarising.

  • Quoting: It is simply copying a short or a long text , from a source into your work
  • Paraphrasing: Using ideas from the source and write in your own words
  • Summarising: Summarising gives a broad overview of an information source.It describes the main ideas own words.
